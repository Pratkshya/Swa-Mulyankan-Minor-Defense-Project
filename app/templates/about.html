About Swa-Mulyankan

A comprehensive diagnostic tool for SEE student success

What is Swa-Mulyankan?

Swa-Mulyankan is a web-based diagnostic tool designed to identify learning gaps in SEE (School Examination Examination) students based on the Nepal CDC Specification Grid 2082. Our mission is to empower students with precise, data-driven insights into their academic performance and provide actionable pathways for improvement.

The Problem We Solve

Traditional testing approaches provide only overall scores, leaving students unsure about where they actually need to improve. Swa-Mulyankan goes deeper—we identify learning gaps at the concept level, showing exactly what you need to master to excel in your exams.

The Tri-Core Architecture

Our system operates on a three-stage framework that ensures comprehensive learning gap identification and personalized remediation:

1

📥 Input (Data Structuring)

Raw questions are mapped to CDC units, weighted, and tagged by cognitive level.

  • OCR or manual input conversion
  • CDC unit and standard mapping
  • Weightage assignment for exam importance
  • Cognitive level tagging (Knowledge, Understanding, Application, Higher Ability)
2

⚙️ Logic (Analysis)

Performance is scored by attempts, mastery index, and severity factors.

  • Track correct/incorrect responses and attempt count
  • Calculate mastery index per chapter
  • Compute severity factor to highlight gaps
  • Align results with cognitive level profiles
3

📊 Output (Feedback)

Analysis is transformed into heatmaps and personalized study recommendations.

  • Color-coded gap heatmap
  • Performance breakdown per topic
  • Suggested focus areas and practice targets
  • Progress tracking over multiple quizzes
